What is SafeNightDrive?

Built for Port Elizabeth

SafeNightDrive is a dedicated ride service created to put women's safety, comfort, and peace of mind first. It's launching this August, timed to Women's Month, in direct response to the growing safety concerns women face when travelling at night.

Why it needs to exist

Recent reports of child abductions and gender-based violence across South Africa have shown how unsafe it can be for women and girls to move freely, especially after dark. High prices and unreliable transport push many young women into unsafe choices — SafeNightDrive exists as the reliable, consistent alternative.

02
Stops along the way

Where we take you

Two ways to ride, both built around one same trusted driver mindset.

i.

Clubbing & Nightlife

We're partnering with local clubs and event venues to become their preferred transport option — helping women get home safely after a big night out, and cutting the risk of getting lost, harassed, or stuck with an unvetted driver.

ii.

Everyday Transport Contract

Contract-based transport for students who commute daily — ideal for anyone studying in Summerstrand but living elsewhere. Get picked up and dropped off by the same trusted driver every day, on a fixed, consistent package.

03
Your driver

What makes us different

Every detail of the ride is designed around one thing: trust.

Branded vehicle

Recognisable, every time

Every SafeNightDrive vehicle is visibly branded and uniform across the fleet — so you always know it's really us before you get in.

Uniformed drivers

A consistent standard

All drivers wear clean, professional uniforms, keeping the brand — and the experience — reliable from ride to ride.

Trusted drivers

Thoroughly vetted

Every driver is background-checked. We currently use trusted male drivers due to the low availability of female drivers at night, and hope to employ more women as we grow.

Direct line to management

Always reachable

Every car is branded with management's contact number, so any concern can be raised directly and transparently.

Wi-Fi onboard

Stay connected

Every ride comes equipped with Wi-Fi, so you can stay online, stay in touch, and stay entertained on the way home.

Your safety matters

That's the whole point

Every choice above exists for one reason — making sure you get home the way you should: safely.
